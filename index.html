Ralith Milith.

Welcome to Ralith Milith, a society based in Kashmir that aims to promote responsible consumption habits and our motto is simple: Attack the Problem, Not the Person. We understand that frugality can sometimes cause harm to individuals and communities, and that's why we're committed to educating people about mindful consumption practices. We believe that by working together, we can create a more sustainable and just society for all. Our initiatives are geared towards reducing waste and promoting responsible consumption practices. We encourage individuals and organizations to join us in our efforts towards creating a more sustainable future. At Ralith Milith, we believe in the power of collaboration and community. Let's work together to make a positive impact and create a better tomorrow. Join us today and let's make a difference!

What we are doing

We Are In A Mission To Create a Drug Free Society.

Social Work

We promote drug abuse awareness education and prevention combat negative impacts on individuals, families, and communities. We reduce drug abuse, provide resources, and support those affected to live healthy, fulfilling lives.

No Drugs

We promote a drug-free lifestyle for better health, relationships, and community involvement.Our goal is to reduce drug abuse prevalence and inspire positive change. We're committed to helping those affected by addiction achieve a fulfilling life.

Health Care

We offer health support to drug abusers through counseling, medication-assisted treatment, and access to medical and mental health services. Our goal is to improve overall health and achieve long-term recovery.

About our Foundation

We Are In A Mission To Create a Drug Free Society.

We are driven by a steadfast commitment to the mission of creating a drug-free society. Our team comprises passionate individuals who are dedicated to the cause and bring a wealth of experience and expertise in combating drug abuse. We believe that every individual has the right to lead a healthy, productive, and fulfilling life, free from the destructive impact of substance abuse. Through our programs, initiatives, and partnerships, we strive to raise awareness about the dangers of drug abuse, empower communities, and support those on the path to recovery. We recognize that the road ahead is challenging, but we remain undeterred in our efforts to create a world where drugs are not a barrier to personal and social progress. Together, we can make a difference, and we invite all stakeholders to join us on this crucial journey.
